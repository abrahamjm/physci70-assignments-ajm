final project ideas

Virtual Head

An immersive audio and video capture dummy head with microphone elements in binaural ears and stereoscopic widefield eyesite in order to attempt to satisfyingly capture and record live audience-participatory theater, specifically original performance shakespeare.


Podorythmie Shoes

Shoes with, hopefully, veolcity sensitive midi triggers in the heel and toe to rhythmically control drum machines or other midi hardwear while playing another instrument simultaneously (Electrotrad). If possible, I'd love to tie velocity not only to loudness but also to other elements like pitch, sustain, or even effects control. I'm also curious about the possibility of adding more sensor elements, like location, to add more control over the sound.


Blood Shirt

A pressure sensitive light-enabled shirt that could create stylized digital wound designs based on contact during stage combat. I'm excited to see if it could distinquish between stabs and gashes.
